## About This Viz

This is the individual counterpart to the portal overview. Each senior gets a full profile card including her complete career journey, a before-and-after stat breakdown, a 2025–2026 season scoring arc, key highlights, and a direct quote from Coach Yo. A side-by-side compare mode lets you put any two seniors head-to-head.

---

## What's Inside Each Profile

- **Career Journey** — Full timeline from high school through every college stop to Ole Miss
- **The Portal Effect** — Points, rebounds, assists, and FG% before and after the transfer
- **Season Scoring Arc** — Game-by-game scoring visualization for 2025–2026
- **Season Highlights** — Key stats and career milestones
- **By the Numbers** — Full stat table comparing previous school vs. Ole Miss
- **In Coach Yo's Words** — Verified quotes from Ole Miss Athletics press releases and postgame pressers
- **Next Chapter** — Post-senior year outlook
